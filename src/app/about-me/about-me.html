About Me

Hi! I'm Stacy, a full-stack software developer who's taken the scenic route to find my dream career. After spending fifteen-plus years building a successfull career in the insurance industry, I knew I needed a change. That's when I discovered a passion for programming that completely reshaped my career goals. What started as a hobby quickly became a calling, and I eventually made the leap into software development the full-stack program at Grand Circus Bootcamp.

Today, in my career so far, I've had the opportunity to work with tools like Angular, React, Typescript, Java, REST APIs, and SQL to build intuitive, scalable applications that make people's live more interesting and easier. I love the challenge of turning complex problems into clean, thoughtful solutions. Most of my experience has centered around frontend architecture, user experience, and creating products that feel effortless to use.

But the heart of who I am isn't just code. I'm a wife to a loving supportive husband, and a mother to two wonderful kids, and they're a big part of what motivates me to keep learning, growing, and building things that genuinely help people. When I'm not working, you'll likely find me outside or curled up on the coach with a good book. I'm happiest camping or biking in the summer, sitting around a bonfire in the fall, or sledding with my family in the winter. Michigan weather keeps things interesting, and I love every season for what it brings.

My journey into tech may not have been a direct path, but it has given me a unique blend of empathy, business maturity, and technical skills. I know what it's like to navigate change, learn fast, and never give up, and that perspective shapes every problem I approach.

If you're curious about my work or want to collaborate, I'd love to connect.
